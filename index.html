About Us

Mr. Raymond Tu, Managing Director, founded the ASE Air Ses Express Limited in 1984. In his previous career he gained management experience with Sea-Land Service Inc., Maersk Lines and others. He had been elected Chairman for two consecutive terms of IOFFLAT (Sea Freight Forwarders Association) and the Director of TAFA (Air Freight Forwarders Association) in Taiwan since 1995, and he is still entitled the honorary advisor in both associations now. “Over the past two decades we have developed an in-depth expertise of the local market allowing us to gain a good reputation and establish a solid link with international freight forwarders, logistics providers, shipping liners, airlines, shippers and consignees, ship owners and operators, cargo clients, governmental units, customs and port authorities, and terminal operators. We believe that customer's satisfaction is not only our business but also our pleasure. Working in a service business we are eager to show you our wholehearted dedication.” We have started our serving operation set-up in China (18) and Vietnam (3) operation besides of Taiwan (4) since 1990 simultaneously to keep on the pace of economic growth to serve our clients and working partners as a key player for a total solution in freight and logistics business over the East Asia area.
